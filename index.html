About SWARG Encryption

SWARG implements genuine AES-256-GCM (Advanced Encryption Standard with 256-bit key in Galois/Counter Mode), the same encryption standard used by government agencies, financial institutions, and security professionals worldwide.

All cryptographic operations are performed locally in your browser using the Web Crypto API. No data is transmitted to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.
